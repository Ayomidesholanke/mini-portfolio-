From Curiosity to Code: A Journey of Discovery

About Me

Welcome to my tech journey! Every great journey begins with curiosity, and mine was no different. My interest in frontend development started from a simple fascination: the beauty of websites. I often found myself wondering how interactive designs and seamless user experiences were created. I didn't know the answers then, but I was determined to uncover them. As a curious beginner, I started exploring the world of tech, which felt both exciting and overwhelming. The first step was researching about frontend development, then i proceed to learning HTML and CSS. It was incredible to see how lines of code could bring ideas to life on a browser. With each tag, style, and layout, I realized how much creativity and logic were intertwined in frontend development.

My Progress

Joining the She Code Africa Bootcamp has been a transformative experience for me, both technically and personally. Through the program, I've gained hands-on experience in core frontend development skills like HTML and CSS, building different projects and hosting projects on platforms like Netlify. The structured lessons and guidance from mentors have taught me discipline, consistency, and problem-solving, especially in debugging and ensuring responsiveness. As I continue the bootcamp, I'm determined to deepen my JavaScript knowledge and other aspects of frontend development, and also prepare for real-world projects, with the goal of becoming a skilled frontend developer. The journey so far has been incredible, and I'm excited for what lies ahead.
